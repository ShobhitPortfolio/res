Welcome to RPS

An Established Leader in Quality Education

Rawal Public School is an established leader in developing and providing education to students. Our aim is to create a community of well-groomed individuals, where a spirit of cooperation and mutual respect exists among students and teachers.

We impart quality education rooted in traditional values, equipping students with skills to thrive and grow into responsible citizens — serving the people of this land and the entire world.

  • CBSE Affiliated — Grades I to XII
  • State-of-the-art infrastructure & smart classrooms
  • Integrated JEE / NEET coaching from Class XI
  • Extensive sports, arts & cultural programmes
  • Safe, nurturing and inclusive campus environment
